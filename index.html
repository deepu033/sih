Welcome to SwasthyaSetu

A project demo that centralizes information about government medical schemes for poor and vulnerable populations. Use the tabs above to explore schemes, find empanelled hospitals, upload your medical reports locally (preview), and compare medicine prices.

Why use this platform?

  • Centralized scheme information (PM-JAY, PMBJP, state schemes)
  • Find empanelled hospitals that accept cashless treatment
  • Compare Janaushadhi & private medicine prices
  • Upload and store medical reports locally on your device
